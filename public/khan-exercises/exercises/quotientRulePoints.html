Suppose that:

f(X) = Y1
f'(X) = M1
g(X) = Y2
g'(X) = M2

What is \left.\dfrac{d}{dx}(\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)})\right|_{x=X} ?

(Y2*M1-Y1*M2)/(Y2*Y2)

The quoteint rule says (\dfrac{f}{g})'(x) = \dfrac{g(x)f'(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}

Plug in x = X

(\dfrac{f}{g})'(X) = \dfrac{g(X)f'(X) - f(X)g'(X)}{g(X)^2}

(\dfrac{f}{g})'(X) = \dfrac{(Y2)(M1) - (Y1)(M2)}{(Y2)^2}

(\dfrac{f}{g})'(X) = \dfrac{toFraction((Y2*M1-Y1*M2)/(Y2*Y2))[0]}{toFraction((Y2*M1-Y1*M2)/(Y2*Y2))[1]}
